Privacy Policy

This site is designed to process most user-provided data in the browser. Site owners should customise this page before production deployment.

Information handled

Most tool inputs are processed client-side and are not intentionally transmitted to a server by the default static implementation. Standard hosting logs, analytics, or ad scripts may still collect technical browsing data depending on deployment choices.

Cookies and analytics

Theme preference is stored locally in the browser. If analytics or advertising services are later added, this policy should be updated to disclose cookies, consent handling, and data retention practices.

User responsibility

Users should avoid pasting regulated or highly sensitive data into any online tool unless they control the deployment environment and understand the privacy posture of that deployment.
